Brief description

This description deals with a mechanism to control the laser3D on the Atlascar.

System operation

The system is designed to rotate a 2D laser about a shaft which is perpendicular to the scannerâ€™s internal mirror axle. The scanner is in forward looking position because it is to be installed onboard a robot with a prevalent sense of motion. To rotate the external shaft, a stepper motor is used with its angular velocity controlled by an MCU. In order to establish connection between the rotating scanner and the fixed part of the platform, a slip ring with adequate signal to noise ratio and power capability has been installed. [R.Pascoal]
